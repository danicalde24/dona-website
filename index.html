Creative Direction · Cultural Platform · Miami Beach

The New Current

Ritz-Carlton South Beach × Art Basel

The Brief

The Ritz-Carlton South Beach needed an Art Basel presence with a real point of view — not another installation, but a cultural moment that reflected where the hotel is going.

What We Ran

Creative direction and concepting. Built The New Current — a platform connecting art, architecture, resilience, and the future of Miami Beach. Coordinated Loris Cecchini's sculpture from Italy to Miami. Curated artists: Marcel Van Luit, Loris Cecchini, Leandro Erlich, The Brave New Earth.

The Results

300M+ impressions across media and social.

Meet the founder

Daniela Calderon

A creative strategist born in Cali, Colombia and based in Miami. Drawn to fashion, music, art, and cultural experiences that make people feel something, she founded Doña to create work with emotional and cultural impact.

Obsessed with storytelling through visuals, atmosphere, and connection, Daniela believes the smallest details shape the way a brand is remembered. Her work is rooted in creating the right energy, bringing the right people together, and building experiences that stay with you long after the moment ends.
